Since 2018, I have been a volunteer graphic designer for the Colorado Environmental Film Festival. This role has allowed me to indulge in my passion for environmental action and motivating audiences, as well as fine-tune my graphic design skills and practice creativity. Throughout these two years, I have helped with both web and print materials, ranging from tickets, schedules, posters, and in 2020, the 44-page festival program. I have also assisted with educating other festival committee members in Adobe Illustrator and InDesign basics. I work closely with Phoebe Ferguson, Dana Coehlo, and Nicole Bickford on festival collateral.
