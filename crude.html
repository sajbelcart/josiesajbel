During my cartography internship at S&P Global Platts, I was tasked with creating a commercial wall map of the North American crude oil system. I used ArcMap, Illustrator, MAPublisher, and InDesign to create the final product (42" by 62"). These are snapshots of certain areas of the map, and the final version is available for purchase. I collaborated with Ginny Mason and Armand Van Wanseele on this project. This map received an honorable mention in cartographic design in the CaGIS 2018 competition.
